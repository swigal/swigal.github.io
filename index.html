Join us in Worship.

Worship offers an avenue of connection to those who are just beggining to reconnect, as well as those who have been working at it for a number of years.

Early Worship

A casual traditional worship service held at 8:15 on Sunday morning.

The Gathering

A modern take on the Methhod of worship, held at 9:30 on Sunday morning. Featuring contemporary Praise and Worship music led by our worship team.

Traditional Worship

A traditionally formal worship service held at 10:45 on Sunday morning. It features all of the traditions set forth by hte Methdist church at its start.

Prayer and Worship

Prayer is a powerful tool, the most powerful tool that God gave us. One that is sometimes neglected. Join us as we seek to worship God through our prayers. We read the scriptures and meditate on them as we pray over our special needs. Join us on hte fourth Wednesday of each month at 6:00 pm for a special Prayer and Worship Service.

Opportunities for Spiritual Growth

Stout Memorial Has many groups that meet to encourage our spiritual growth. We have three adult Sunday School classes that meet on Sunday Mornings, as well as three youth and children's classes. The Journey and K.I.D.S Life meet on Sundays as well. On Wednesday morning we have a morning there is a Bible study group that meets at 10:00 am and we have several small roups that meet throughout the month. If you would like more information, just go to our "Growth Opportunites" page. You can find all of the information there.

As another method of spiritual growth, we are now featuring an audio recording of the pastor's sermon on our website. All of the pastor's sermons will be posted in the "Pastor's Blog". You can find this page by clicking the button above or under the HOME menu at the top of every page. Each week we will post the audio as well as the scriptre that goes with the sermon.
We hope you enjoy listening to the pastor's sermons and that you would share them with your friends on social media.
